A mandatory advance payment is required to commence development. Once work begins, advance payments are strictly non-refundable as they cover immediate time, labor, and resources allocated to your project.

Estimated timelines begin only after the client has provided all necessary content (text, logos, images). Any significant changes to the project scope after commencement may incur additional development charges.
